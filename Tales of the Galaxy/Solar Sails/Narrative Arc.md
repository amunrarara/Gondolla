### The Dreams of Ani
Ani recounts his childhood dreams as he sits in a dry riverbed and looks at the stars.

Transition: The Solar Sailor falls from the sky

### Solar Sailor
The Sailor's fall makes a crater in The Glass Flats. Ani waits with the Sailor until daybreak, when the villagers, and The Scribes, come to investigate the "fallen star".

Transition: Ani is pushed out from the crater.
