Ádia, Language of the Adáni

---

**áti** - animal; any being with bodies physical, etheric, and astral. Plural: **átu**.

**péri** - plant; any being with bodies physical and etheric. Plural: **péru**.

**méte** - mineral; any inert physical substance
	*mérki /* a chunk of copper
	*merkû*, / 2 or more chunks of copper.

---

**Ádani** - The Starfarers, travellers between worlds; a being with bodies physical, etheric, and astral, united with an I and capable of active, conscious participation in higher worlds. "Little God who chooses." - Plural: **Adáne**

**Vándri** - Singular; a Being of Lesser Light, guardians of the Adáni; akin to Angels, Spirits -  Plural: **Vandréi**

**Ánavandri** - Singular; a Being of Greater Light; controller of powerful forces; akin to Archangels - Plural: **Anávandrei**
	also **Ánavand, Anvándranei**

--- 

**ílyë** - the exosphere of a planet; solarsails begin their journeys through the Galaxy by rising from  the ilyë of a planet.

**ilyáneia** - the space between stars, where "cool waters flow gently"

**árcha** - air, oxygen, the space between ílyë and béna

**béna** - earth, soil, solid, stout, the space between árcha and kuthíthi

---

**kúthi** - heart

**bûn** - blood, river, liquid

**kuthíthi** - planet core

---

**níma** - stillness, rest, silence, the word for "noun"

**ánima** - activity, not stillness, the word for "verb"

**énti** - form, shape, type

---

**eía** - sight, to see, is in sight

**áneia** - blindness, cannot see, not in sight
